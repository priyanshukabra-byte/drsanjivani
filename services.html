The Engagement

One package. One expert. One crop cycle.

We assess your land, recommend the right crops, and guide you through the full growing season — one field visit per month, for up to 6 months or one complete crop cycle, whichever comes first.

What's Included

The Dr. Sanjivani Consultancy Package

Agricultural Consultancy Engagement

From first soil test to harvest guidance — one continuous engagement with one assigned agronomist

Every Month

  • 1 full-day field visit by your assigned agronomist
  • Soil and crop observations documented
  • Written report delivered after every visit
  • Recommendations updated based on crop progress
  • WhatsApp access to your agronomist throughout

Over the Engagement

  • Soil health analysis: pH, N-P-K, micronutrients, organic carbon
  • Water source and irrigation feasibility assessment
  • Crop and variety recommendation — matched to your land
  • Month-by-month planting calendar
  • Input advisory: fertilisers, amendments, quantities, timing
  • Yield and income estimates for recommended crops
  • R&D insights from Basant Agro Tech's field trials where relevant
  • Full written record of every recommendation and visit

What We Don't Do

  • Provide or manage farm labour
  • Sell seeds or fertilisers
  • Run day-to-day farm operations
  • Guarantee yield outcomes
  • Continue beyond the crop cycle or 6 months
We can recommend labour, vendors, input suppliers, and equipment — but we do not arrange or manage them. The client or their existing team executes. Our job ends with the advice.

The Journey

From first call to final report

1

You reach out

WhatsApp, email, or the contact form. A 20-minute call to understand your land — size, location, current state, what you want from it.

2

Proposal & confirmation

Fixed fee proposal shared within 48 hours. You confirm and pay. First field visit date is scheduled.

3

Month 1 — First field visit

Your agronomist visits the land. Soil samples collected, full assessment conducted. Crop and variety recommendation delivered in a written report within 7 days.

4

Months 2 onwards — Monthly visits

One field visit per month. Written update after each. WhatsApp access throughout for urgent questions. Guidance adjusted based on crop progress at every visit.

5

End of engagement

At the close of the crop cycle or 6 months. You receive a complete record of every visit, every recommendation, and every outcome observed. The land is yours to carry forward.
